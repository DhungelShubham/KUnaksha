About Us

Welcome to KU Naksha, your ultimate guide to Kathmandu University campus. Our project aims to provide an interactive, easy-to-navigate map of the university's infrastructure, including academic buildings, halls, and recreational areas.

Whether you're a student, faculty, or visitor, KU Naksha is designed to help you find your way around the campus effortlessly. With detailed map markers, you can quickly locate important buildings such as Block 10, Block 9, Ciku Hall, and KU Corner.

This project was created by a passionate team of developers and designers from Kathmandu University, dedicated to improving the campus experience through technology.

Our goal is to make KU Naksha a user-friendly, intuitive, and accurate map that supports both new visitors and long-time members of the university community. We continuously update the map to include new locations and improvements.
